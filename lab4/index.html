Task One

How to progagate a houseplant:

  • step one: find a plant you want to progagate
  • step two: find a clean and sharp pair of scissors
  • step three: find a piece of the plant that you want to progagate
  • step four: look for the node(a little root) on that section of the plant
  • step five: cut above the node at least half an inch
  • step six: take the cutting and place it in vase of clean water
  • step seven: place in a spot with bright indirect light
  • step eight: watch the roots grow and enjoy your new houseplant
